# Skilly 90-Day Zero-Budget Execution Plan

## Focus

- Market: English-speaking global developer audience
- ICP: indie developers and technical founders
- Goal: maximize organic download clicks
- Time budget: 6-8 hours/week

## Success Criteria

- Organic sessions: +200% vs baseline
- Website visit -> download click conversion: >= 6%
- Mentions per week (not from founder): >= 3
- Ranking goals: 20 keywords in top 30; 5 keywords in top 10

## Phase 1 (Weeks 1-2): Foundation

### Deliverables

- Product positioning and messaging consistency document
- Website route structure live:
  - `/blog/[slug]`
  - `/guides/[topic]`
  - `/vs/[alternative]`
  - `/use-cases/[persona]-[job]`
  - `/templates/[workflow]`
- Structured data live:
  - `SoftwareApplication`
  - `FAQPage`
  - `BreadcrumbList`
  - `Article`
- Tracking events and UTM convention operational

### Week 1 checklist

- Collect baseline metrics
- Publish pillar #1
- Launch one glossary/definitions page
- Push one GitHub release/discussion update

### Week 2 checklist

- Publish pillar #2
- Publish one comparison page
- Validate internal links and sitemap coverage

## Phase 2 (Weeks 3-4): Cold Start Distribution

### Deliverables

- Weekly publishing cadence stabilized
- First Show HN launch
- 2 Reddit high-value threads/comments per week
- 1 Dev.to mirrored post per week

### Week 3 checklist

- Publish pillar #3
- Repurpose into:
  - 3 short posts
  - 1 long-form post
  - 1 comparison angle post
  - 1 checklist post

### Week 4 checklist

- Review channel CTR + download contribution
- Drop underperforming content format if <1% CTR

## Phase 3 (Weeks 5-8): Repeatable Growth

### Deliverables

- Comparison and use-case page expansion
- Product Hunt launch prep and execution
- Lightweight programmatic SEO templates activated

### Weekly deliverable floor

- 1 SEO article
- 3 short X posts
- 1 long X thread
- 2 Reddit interactions
- 1 GitHub update

## Phase 4 (Weeks 9-12): Optimization

### Deliverables

- Keep winners, cut low-return channels
- Refresh top pages with updated internal links
- Iterate homepage and download-section CTA
- Expand high-performing content clusters only

### End-of-cycle review

- Compare baseline vs week 12 for all KRs
- Build next 90-day plan from top 20% winning actions
